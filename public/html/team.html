    Dr. Matthew McInnes,

    DDS

    Dr. McInnes grew up in South Jordan and graduated from Bingham High School. He was involved in football, track and field and student government. After high school, he attended Brigham Young University for a year before serving an LDS church mission in Portugal. Returning from Portugal, Dr. McInnes continued his studies at BYU in accounting at the Marriot School of Management as well as completing his dental prerequisites. Dr. McInnes then moved to Southern California to begin dental school at Loma Linda University. Being a Christian university, Dr. McInnes appreciated the standards and morals belonging to his fellow students and professors as well as their desire to fulfill the school's mission to help make man whole. While in California, Dr. McInnes met his wife, Michele. Now with three boys, the presence of cars, trucks, motorcycles and balls are plentiful. When not at the office, Dr. McInnes can be found playing with his sons, working in the yard, riding ATV's and watching his sons at their baseball, soccer and basketball games. Dr. McInnes has been fortunate to be surrounded by great team members, all wanting to make your experience here as pleasant as possible and hopefully find a friend. We will make every effort to answer your questions and make you comfortable at each visit. Dr. McInnes enjoys dentistry and the benefits it brings to all of our lives. From something as simple as a routine check-up to relieving a painful tooth or to making a new smile, he enjoys it all and looks forward to being able to spend time with you.

      DDS

      Dr. J. Shea Bess is a Utah native and loves the state. He was born and raised in the Salt Lake Valley and graduated from Bingham High School in 1991. He married a beautiful South Jordan girl in 1994. He attended the University of Utah and obtained a bachelor’s degree in medical biology. He then moved on to dental school at Loma Linda University in southern California. Loma Linda University is owned by the Seventh Day Adventist Church, and he enjoyed the people and appreciated their strong value system. Dr. Bess' first son was born while in dental school and he now has four great boys. They are his focus in life and he loves spending time with his wife and kids, especially outdoors!
