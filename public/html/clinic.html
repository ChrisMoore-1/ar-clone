      Our
      mission

      INTEGRITY-We treat our patients as we would want to be treated ourselves. We speak in straightforward honesty, and use words that patients can understand.

      Trust is earned, not expected. If we earn our patients trust, we can assist them through any dental concern they ever encounter. We provide options we would consider ourselves.

      We tell our patients what we would do if their mouth was ours, and then allow them to choose what they want.

      The patient is always in control. We don't appreciate high pressure, therefore, it will not be found here.

      History

      Founded in 2008 – by Dr. Matt McInnes – our dental clinic is enjoying an explosive growth in popularity not only among South Jordan, but also among residents of other regions of the Salt Lake County. Our dental clinic offers many services, ranging from general to cosmetic and emergency dentistry.

      At our dental clinic, we strive to provide impeccable dental care to each and everyone, regardless of financial circumstances. Hence the development of customized financing arrangements. Call or visit our dental clinic to learn more!
